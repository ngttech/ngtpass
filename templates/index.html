Password Tips

  • Keep Your Passwords Private: Only share your passwords with trusted individuals, such as a spouse or partner, if absolutely necessary. Avoid sharing them with others.
  • Use Unique Passwords: Create different passwords for each of your online accounts. This helps protect your information if one account is compromised.
  • Manage Your Passwords Safely: If you need help remembering your passwords, consider using a password manager. This is a secure tool that stores all your passwords in one place, protected by a single master password. Alternatively, you can write them down in a secure, private location, such as a locked drawer or a safe.
